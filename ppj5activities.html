Liu Yifei Activities

Tissot

Liu Yifei is a woman of many talents, being an actor, singer and model. A graduate of the Beijing Film Academy, the Chinese actor first came to the public’s attention in television films and series such as The Legend of Sword and Fairy, which was when an entire nation fell under her spell. Hollywood spotted her, too, which led to her playing opposite Jackie Chan, Jet Li and Nicolas Cage. More recently, she was chosen by Disney to play Mulan, the legendary warrior. Tissot is especially proud to see her representing “La Locle-style” time!
